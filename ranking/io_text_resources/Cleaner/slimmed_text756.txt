Ask me anything Archive Brazil 101 We\'re on Twitter! 2020 Oct 7 | \xc2\xa9 Brazil\xe2\x80\x99s economic priority should be jobs, not fiscal disciplineBrazil won plaudits from investors by insisting its most pressing economic challenge is tackling a record deficit caused by COVID-19, but a growing number of economists say the government\xe2\x80\x99s priority should be getting millions of Brazilians back to work.Figures last week showed the grim state of Brazil\xe2\x80\x99s labor market. Unemployment and underemployment rates are the highest on record; so is the number of people who have given up looking for work and dropped out of the labor force.The resulting slack is suffocating consumer demand for goods and services, discouraging firms from investing, and is a long-term drag on economic growth, economists say.Brazil\xe2\x80\x99s $1.3 trillion economy is set to shrink a record 5% this year and will grow by just 3.5% next year, according to the latest forecast from a weekly central bank poll of economists.An economy ministry report last week entitled \xe2\x80\x98Long-Term Economic Growth Strategy\xe2\x80\x99 - issued in response to a local media critique of its handling of the economy - highlighted where the government\xe2\x80\x99s focus lies.The word \xe2\x80\x98fiscal\xe2\x80\x99 appeared six times, and there were six references made to the government\xe2\x80\x99s \xe2\x80\x98reform\xe2\x80\x99 agenda. There was not a single mention of \xe2\x80\x98jobs\xe2\x80\x99, \xe2\x80\x98employment\xe2\x80\x99 or \xe2\x80\x98work\xe2\x80\x99.Brazil is not alone in spending big to minimize economic pain from the pandemic. But a weak fiscal starting point means government debt is on course to end this year around 95% of GDP, way higher than the Latin American &amp; Caribbean average of 59%, according to Fitch.Few dispute that the record deficit must be reversed at some point. Investors are growing nervous and Brazilian markets are under increasing strain.Yet the International Monetary Fund said on Monday that Brazil\xe2\x80\x99s near-term policy priority should be \xe2\x80\x9csaving lives and livelihoods\xe2\x80\x9d.
