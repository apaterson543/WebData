Hope for a vaccine\n</h4><div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>World Health Organization (WHO) Director-General, Dr Tedros Adhanom Ghebreyesus, said yesterday <a href="https://uk.reuters.com/article/uk-health-coronavirus-who-board/covid-19-vaccine-may-be-ready-by-year-end-says-whos-tedros-idUKKBN26R2C0?il=0" target="_blank" rel="noopener">a COVID-19 vaccine could be ready by the end of the year</a>. </p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>\xe2\x80\x9cWe will need vaccines and there is hope that by the end of this year we may have a vaccine. There is hope,\xe2\x80\x9d Tedros said in final remarks to the WHO\xe2\x80\x99s Executive Board, without elaborating.</p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>There are currently nine experimental vaccines in the WHO\'s COVAX global vaccine programme. </p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>Tedros\'s remarks come as an experimental <a href="https://uk.reuters.com/article/uk-health-coronavirus-china-vaccine/chinas-experimental-covid-19-vaccine-appears-safe-study-idUKKBN26S0FM?il=0" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Chinese COVID-19 vaccine is shown to be safe</a> in an early stage clinical trial - although the study is yet to be peer reviewed. China has at least four experimental vaccines in the final stage of clinical trials, reports Reuters.</p>\n</div>\n  <div class="st__content-block st__content-block--snippet st__content-block--snippet-default">\n    <div class="snippet-content js-snippet-content">\n      <div class="snippet-content__header">\n        <span class="snippet-content__caption"></span>\n        <h4 class="snippet-content__title">What is the COVID Response Alliance for Social Entrepreneurship?</h4>\n      </div>\n      <div class="snippet-content__body">\n        <div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>The COVID Response Alliance for Social Entrepreneurship is hosted by the Schwab Foundation for Social Entrepreneurship, with the support of Yunus Social Business and GHR Foundation and integrates the perspectives of social entrepreneurs through a strategic partnership with Catalyst 2030.</p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>It identifies five principles that should lie at the heart of any COVID-19 response effort:</p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--list">\n    <ul>\n        <li>Make the voices of social entrepreneurs and their communities heard</li>\n        <li>Prepare to respond, recover and reset \xe2\x80\x93 taking the opportunity to \xe2\x80\x9cshape a new tomorrow\xe2\x80\x9d </li>\n        <li>Collaborate across sectors in recognition of the complexity and scale of the crisis </li>\n        <li>Look beyond healthcare, given that COVID-19 touches all areas of people\xe2\x80\x99s lives </li>\n        <li>Support the shovel-ready solutions that grassroots organizations are already able to provide today <br> </li>\n    </ul>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>The Alliance has released a COVID Social Enterprise Action Agenda, outlining 25 concrete recommendations for key stakeholder groups to support social entrepreneurs during COVID-19. These align around the following streams:</p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>1. <b>Intermediaries and networks</b> to surface the needs of the social entrepreneurs they serve on the ground and provide them with fitting support<br>2. <b>(Impact) investors</b> to adapt their investment priorities and processes, and provide flexible capital and must-have technical assistance <br>3. <b>Corporations</b> to stand with the social entrepreneurs in their supply chains and ecosystems, and join forces with them to \xe2\x80\x9cshape a new tomorrow\xe2\x80\x9d <br>4. <b>Funders and philanthropists</b> to expand and expedite their financial support to social entrepreneurs and intermediaries, taking risks reflective of today\xe2\x80\x99s unprecedented times <br>5. <b>Government institutions</b> at all levels to recognize social entrepreneurs as a driving force in safeguarding jobs and in building a greener and equitable society, and to back them accordingly<br> <br> <br></p>\n</div>\n\n      </div>\n      <div class="snippet-content__footer">\n        <button class="snippet-content__button js-snippet-content__button" data-hide-text="Hide" data-show-text="Show"><span>Show</span></button>\n      </div>\n    </div>\n  </div>\n<h4 class="st__content-block st__content-block--heading st__content-block--heading-4" id="3-a-warning-from-the-imf">\n  3. A warning from the IMF\n</h4><div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>The Managing Director of the International Monetary Fund, Kristalina Georgieva, has warned about the <a rel="noopener">damage that will be caused by removing fiscal and monetary support too soon</a>. </p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>\xe2\x80\x9cWe are very clear in the message we are communicating to not withdraw support prematurely,\xe2\x80\x9d Georgieva said at an online event marking the 125th anniversary of the London School of Economics. \xe2\x80\x9cIf we do so, then we risk massive bankruptcies and massive unemployment.\xe2\x80\x9d</p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block st__content-block--text">\n    <p>Debt service suspension for poor countries must also be continued, while tax reform will be critical to help countries service their debt obligations, she added. </p>\n</div>\n<div class="st__content-block
