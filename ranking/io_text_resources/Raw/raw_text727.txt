Order a home test kit if you cannot get to a test site.</p><h3>In England:</h3><ul><li>you need to get the test done in the first 8 days of having symptoms</li><li>on days 1 to 7, you can get tested at a site or at home \xe2\x80\x93 if you&#x27;re ordering a home test kit on day 7, do it by 3pm</li><li>on day 8, you need to go to a test site \xe2\x80\x93 it&#x27;s too late to order a home test kit</li></ul><h3>In Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland:</h3><ul><li>you need to get the test done in the first 5 days of having symptoms</li><li>on days 1 to 4, you can get tested at a site or at home \xe2\x80\x93 if you&#x27;re ordering a home test kit on day 4, do it by 3pm</li><li>on day 5, you need to go to a test site \xe2\x80\x93 it&#x27;s too late to order a home test kit</li></ul>\n  \n    <div class="nhsuk-inset-text">\n  <span class="nhsuk-u-visually-hidden">Information: </span>\n  <h3>Stay at home if you have symptoms</h3><p>You must stay at home (<a href="https://www.nhs.uk/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/self-isolation-and-treatment/when-to-self-isolate-and-what-to-do/">self-isolate</a>) if you have symptoms, whether or not you get a test. Find out <a href="/conditions/coronavirus-covid-19/self-isolation-and-treatment/how-long-to-self-isolate/">how long to self-isolate</a>.</p><p>Anyone in your support bubble must also stay at home. If you cannot get a test now, or the location or time are not convenient, try again in a few hours.</p>\n</div>\n\n  \n    <p>Get a test online on GOV.UK.</p>\n  \n    \n\n<a href="https://self-referral.test-for-coronavirus.service.gov.uk/antigen/condition" role="button" draggable="false" class="nhsuk-button">\n  Start now on the GOV.UK website\n</a>\n\n  \n    <p></p><h2>Get help applying</h2><p>If you have problems using the online service, call:</p><ul><li>119 if you&#x27;re in England, Wales or Northern Ireland</li><li>0300 303 2713 if you&#x27;re in Scotland</li></ul><p>Lines are open 7am to 11pm.</p>\n  \n    <div class="nhsuk-inset-text">\n  <span class="nhsuk-u-visually-hidden">Information: </span>\n  <p>If no tests are available online, do not call the helpline to get a test.
