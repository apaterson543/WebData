That includes an uptick in such favorites as pizza (55 percent), hamburgers (48 percent), ice cream (46 percent), French fries (45 percent), mac and cheese (38 percent), spaghetti and meatballs (32 percent) and others.</p>                  <div id="div-gpt-ad-sidebar-mrect-mobile" class="advertisement"></div>\n\r\n\r\n<p>The survey was conducted by OnePoll in conjunction with Farm Rich, and it looked at American eating habits during the pandemic.
