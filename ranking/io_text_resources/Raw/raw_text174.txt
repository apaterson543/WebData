<div class="e-article-text" js-article-text>\n        \n          \n            <p>A longtime Jersey Shore food bank employee died Monday of complications from the coronavirus.</p><p>Diana Tennant, 51, a mother of three from Bradley Beach worked for Fulfill \xe2\x80\x94 formerly known as The FoodBank of Monmouth and Ocean Counties \xe2\x80\x94 for a dozen years.\xc2\xa0</p><p>\xe2\x80\x9cIt is an incredibly sad day at Fulfill, as we lost a champion for the hungry, a dedicated colleague, and a dear friend,\xe2\x80\x9d said Kim Guadagno, Fulfill CEO and president and former lieutenant governor of New Jersey.</p><div class="b-banner m-mobile-rectangle m-inline" data-position="mobile_rectangle" js-banner=""></div>\n<p>Guadagno said that Tennant had a long history with Fulfill, starting as a volunteer and donor, and then a SNAP advocate. She became an employee 12 years ago, and most recently worked as part of Fulfill\'s Resources Connections team.\xc2\xa0</p><p>\xe2\x80\x9cThere are many heroes of this coronavirus crisis\xe2\x80\xa6nurses, doctors, police officers, firefighters, EMTS, and, like Diana, food bankers on the front lines with the public making sure people who need food and services receive help. Her impact serving the community she adored cannot be underestimated. We will carry on Diana\xe2\x80\x99s mission to feed our hungry neighbors. Our deepest condolences to her family and friends,\xe2\x80\x9d Guadagno said in a statement.</p><div js-nativo=""></div><p>Gov. Phil Murphy paid tribute to Tennant during his Tuesday news briefing.</p><p>\xc2\xa0Murphy noted that Tennant also was a leader within the state\xe2\x80\x99s Latinx community, having served as a chairwoman of the Latino Festival in Monmouth County.</p><div class="b-banner m-mobile-rectangle m-inline" data-position="mobile_rectangle" js-banner=""></div>\n<p>\xe2\x80\x9cShe is remembered by her friends as someone who always had time to lend a hand,\xe2\x80\x9d Murphy said. \xe2\x80\x9cDiana was only 51-years-old and had so much more to give.\xe2\x80\x9d</p>\n          \n        \n\n
