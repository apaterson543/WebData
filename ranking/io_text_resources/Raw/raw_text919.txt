Your conference will get mistaken for a webinar.</h2><p id="eb75" class="gc gd em ge b gf it gg gh gi iu gj gk gl iv gm gn go iw gp gq gr ix gs gt gv cy dd">Before corona, webinars were at times a necessary evil, occurring often enough, but fairly ignored. Your content is competing in a saturated market.</h2><p id="5982" class="gc gd em ge b gf it gg gh gi iu gj gk gl iv gm gn go iw gp gq gr ix gs gt gv cy dd">There\xe2\x80\x99s a lot of content cropping up in the last three months. You\xe2\x80\x99re a conference organizer, not a TV producer.</h2><p id="f4fd" class="gc gd em ge b gf it gg gh gi iu gj gk gl iv gm gn go iw gp gq gr ix gs gt gv cy dd">You may be tempted to assume \xe2\x80\x98it\xe2\x80\x99s basically TV, right?\xe2\x80\x9d I did at first. An Established Purpose</h2><p id="8697" class="gc gd em ge b gf it gg gh gi iu gj gk gl iv gm gn go iw gp gq gr ix gs gt gv cy dd">Before you name your conference, before you start messaging potential panelists \xe2\x80\x94 please state, to yourself and to other stakeholders, the purpose of producing this event and the desired outcome.
