class="breadcrumb"><a href="/home"><span>Home</span></a><span class="breadcrumbSep">&nbsp;\xe2\x80\xba&nbsp;</span><a href="/news"><span>News</span></a><span class="breadcrumbSep">&nbsp;\xe2\x80\xba&nbsp;</span><span class="last_trail">New testing method can diagnose Covid-19 in just 30 minutes, study finds</span></div>\r\n                        <h2 style="letter-spacing:-.5px;">New testing method can diagnose Covid-19 in just 30 minutes, study finds</h2>\r\n                        <div class="row padt15">\r\n                            <div id="divimage" class="col-lg-3">\r\n                               <img src="../../gallery/event/admin_202010070948196236_4vmex1l2.jpg" height="280" width="280"/>\r\n                                </div>\r\n                            <div id="divimagecontent" class="col-lg-9">\r\n                                <p>\r\n\t<span style="color: rgb(51, 51, 51); font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 17px; font-style: italic; font-weight: 700;">The biggest benefit is that a diagnostic kit can be developed within a week even if a new infectious disease appears other than Covid-19, according to the research published in the journal Nature Biomedical Engineering.</span></p>\r\n<div>\r\n\tScientists have developed a new method that allows anyone to easily and quickly detect Covid-19 in just 30 minutes, and is as accurate as the current PCR diagnostic test. The SENSR technology developed by researchers at Pohang University of Science & Technology (POSTECH) in South Korea diagnosis Covid-19 based on the RNA sequence of the virus, reducing the stress on one single testing location and avoiding contact with infected patients as much as possible.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tRNA is a nucleic acid that mediates genetic information or is involved in controlling the expression of genes.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tThe biggest benefit is that a diagnostic kit can be developed within a week even if a new infectious disease appears other than Covid-19, according to the research published in the journal Nature Biomedical Engineering.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tThe researchers noted that PCR molecular test currently used for Covid-19 diagnosis has very high accuracy but entails a complex preparation process to extract or refine the virus.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tThe test is not suitable for use in small farming or fishing villages, or airport or drive-thru screening clinics as it requires expensive equipment as well as skilled experts, they said.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tThe researchers designed the test kit to produce nucleic acid binding reaction to show fluorescence only when Covid-19 RNA is present.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tThe virus can be detected immediately without any preparation process with high sensitivity in a short time. And it is as accurate as the current PCR diagnostic method.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tUsing the new technology, they found the SARS-CoV-2 virus RNA, the cause of Covid-19, from an actual patient sample in about 30 minutes.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tIn addition, five pathogenic viruses and bacterial RNAs were detected which proved the kit&#39;s usability in detecting pathogens other than Covid-19, according to the researchers.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tAnother great advantage of the SENSR technology is the ease of creating the diagnostic device that can be developed into a simple portable and easy-to-use form, the researchers said.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\tThe method not only allows onsite diagnosis before going to the screening clinic or being hospitalised, but also allows for a more proactive response to Covid-19 by supplementing the current centralized diagnostic system.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&quot;This method is a fast and simple diagnostic technology which can accurately analyse the RNA without having to treat a patient&#39;s sample,&quot; said POSTECH Professor Jeong Wook Lee. &quot;We can better prepare for future epidemics as we can design and produce a diagnostic kit for new infectious diseases within a week,&quot; Lee said.</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<div>\r\n\t&nbsp;</div>\r\n<p>\r\n\t&nbsp;</p>\r\n<div>\r\n\tSource:https://bit.ly/36CXwl1</div>\r\n                            </div>\r\n\r\n                             \r\n\r\n\r\n                        </div>\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n\r\n                    </div>\r\n                </div>\r\n               \r\n\r\n            </div>\r\n             \r\n\r\n<div class="moduleFooter">\r\n    <div class="widgets_wrapper">\r\n        <div class="container">\r\n            <div class="one-fourth column">\r\n                <aside id="text-2" class="widget widget_text">\r\n                    <h5>Contact Us</h5>\r\n                    <div class="textwidget">\r\n                        Guwahati<br />\r\n                        1st Floor, Orion Place,Old Post Office\r\n                                G.S.
