The increase is primarily due to a cluster of cases at Syracuse University, county officials said.</p><p class="article__paragraph article__paragraph--left" id="L4EU2TICHFANVKM45UA6NH4DDA">The university<a href="https://www.syracuse.edu/covid-dashboard/"> reported 20 new coronavirus cases</a> among students today, the largest number reported in a single day this semester. University officials have blamed the surge in part on off-campus gatherings, including a <a href="https://www.syracuse.com/coronavirus/2020/10/syracuse-university-blames-surge-in-coronavirus-cases-on-off-campus-party.html" target="_blank">party last week</a> on Walnut Avenue.</p><div class="InContent_Center_Background"><div class="InContent_Center_Padding"><div id="InContent_Center_L4EU2TICHFANVKM45UA6NH4DDA" class="ad ad--in-article"></div></div></div><p class="article__paragraph article__paragraph--left" id="X7DWVQX465HSVLXK5S7ZEI2P4Y">For Onondaga County, the total number of cases since March is now 4,382. The last time there were more than 48 was May 25, when there were 62, said county spokesman Justin Sayles.</p><div class="InContent_Center_Background"><div class="InContent_Center_Padding"><div id="InContent_Center_X7DWVQX465HSVLXK5S7ZEI2P4Y" class="ad-observer"></div></div></div><p class="article__paragraph article__paragraph--left" id="QL3CWKDRQJCFJLZSVWJMBSBUOM">There are 209 active cases in the county, up 34 from Tuesday. A total of 207 county residents have died from the virus.</p><div class="InContent_Center_Background"><div class="InContent_Center_Padding"><div id="InContent_Center_MUX4NWH4TFDZZHYZ7HUT6RELTQ" class="ad-observer"></div></div></div><p class="article__paragraph article__paragraph--left" id="TIPPSIEVTJGFZKDIT5BMIXP66Y">There are 17 patients with Covid-19 in local hospitals today. None is in critical condition, county officials reported.</p><div class="InContent_Center_Background"><div class="InContent_Center_Padding"><div id="InContent_Center_TIPPSIEVTJGFZKDIT5BMIXP66Y"
