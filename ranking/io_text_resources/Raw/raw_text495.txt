accordance with port health protocols has been placed in quarantine for a 14-day period from October 5 2020.\xe2\x80\x9d</p>\n<p>The spokeswoman added: \xe2\x80\x9cEnQuest has a robust policy for the management of COVID-19 on all its installations and at its facilities globally.</p>\n<p>\xe2\x80\x9cThe company has engaged with all relevant stakeholders in this instance, including industry and medical organisations.</p>\n<p>\xe2\x80\x9cThe company continues to work with them to ensure its operational response and advice to its workforce is appropriate and commensurate with the prevailing
