questions from reporters," McEnany said.</p>\n<div class="td-a-rec td-a-rec-id-content_inline  td_uid_2_5f88bee55eb49_rand td_block_template_1"><span class="td-adspot-title">Advertisement</span><!-- consistently for the best protection."</p>\n<p>"Kayleigh briefly removed her mask at the mic to answer questions, was there for two questions and only 58 seconds (would not constitute \'sustained Personnel by CDC &amp; DHS. This means they are expected to continue to work - while taking precautions - until a medical recommendation otherwise is given," the official said.</p>\n<p>McEnany, who has <a href="https://americanindependent.com/kayleigh-mcenany-joe-biden-mask-coronavirus-donald-trump-white-house-covid-19/">defended and echoed</a> Donald Trump\'s anti-mask rhetoric for months, also claimed she "wore my mask diligently."</p>\n<p>Several White House reporters <a href="https://www.axios.com/white-house-coronavirus-cases-reporters-316b4da7-8f98-4973-87fd-0b42561f870a.html">have tested positive</a> for the coronavirus over the last few days.
